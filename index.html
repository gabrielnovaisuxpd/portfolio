Leadership

How I build
design teams.

Coaching over doing

02

Process as leverage

Good rituals — review, critique, discovery — are the infrastructure that lets teams operate at high quality without heroics.

03

Radical candor

Direct feedback, delivered with care. I have the hard conversations early so teams don't waste time on misaligned assumptions.

Cross-functional trust

Design earns influence through partnership. I align closely with Product, Engineering, and Operations to make design strategically relevant.
