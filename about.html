2010 → today

The long way here.

2010 — 2014

Graphic Designer · FuturaIM São Paulo

Where craft started: social content, email marketing, banners, flyers, backdrops. Everything printed or pixelated, always on deadline. I learned to ship daily before I learned to strategize.

2014 — 2017

Sydney, Australia Mercury Colleges

A 6-month English exchange that became almost four years. New language, new culture, new confidence — the detour that made everything after it possible.

2018 — 2019

UI Designer & Freelance UX São Paulo

Back in Brazil: e-commerce design at Venoarte by day, freelance UX for corporations and startups in between — wireframes, prototypes, usability tests. The pivot from making things pretty to making things work.

2019

UX Consultant · HeadMind Partners São Paulo

Consulting for Vivo, Claro, TIM, and Nextel — plus the first taste of financial-market startups. Six months that pointed the compass at fintech.

2019 — 2021

sim;paul → Binance São Paulo

Second UX designer hired at sim;paul, building a brokerage app from the ground up — then acquired by Binance. As Senior Product Designer I led the Investment and Onboarding squads: liveness and OCR onboarding automation, treasury bonds and fixed income flows, fully digital investment portability.

2021 — 2022

Product Design Lead → Design Manager · TradersClub São Paulo

From leading six designers shipping app-based crypto trading and an income-tax platform, to managing 4 coordinators and 20 designers across B2C, B2B, and brokerage units. The year design leadership became the job — hiring, coaching, culture, and quality at scale.

2023 — now

Head of Product Design · TownSq Texas, US · Remote

Leading design strategy for a global HOA platform with 4M+ users across B2C and B2B. Design system, continuous discovery, structured design reviews — and an AI-powered UX practice (AI Suggested Reply, feedback intelligence) that turned operational complexity into measurable NPS gains.

2026 — now

Partner · Sion Life Corretora de Seguros Brazil

Bringing product thinking to a brokerage with a decade of insurance expertise: digital-first client experience, CRM and AI-driven workflows, go-to-market for new segments. Seventeen years of financial product design, applied to a business I co-own.

2026 — now

Angel Investor · Invisible Keyboard Global

Backing a content-strategy company that helps YC-backed founders turn content into trust and inbound across 7+ countries. The thesis: the next generation of B2B companies will be built on founder-led content.
